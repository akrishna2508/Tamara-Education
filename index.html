Prizes & Opportunities

More than prizes — a pathway to real research & global science fairs

🥇

First Prize

Complete project sponsorship by Tamara Foundation, including research design, hardware support, experimentation, documentation, and end-to-end guidance.

🥈

Second Prize

Advanced mentorship from Tamara Education with structured guidance on research methodology, prototyping, and competition preparation.

🥉

Third Prize

Research mentorship and refinement support to help transform promising ideas into publishable or competition-ready projects.

✨ Selected teams may receive long-term guidance toward ISEF and global science fairs.

Eligibility & Details

✅ Who Can Participate?

  • High school students (Grades 6–12)
  • Individual or teams of up to 3 members
  • Open to India and international students
  • Beginners welcome — no prior experience needed

💡 Allowed Topics

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Healthcare & Medical Innovation
  • Robotics & IoT
  • Environmental Sustainability
  • Social Impact Solutions

📋 Judging Criteria

  • Innovation & originality of idea
  • Scientific methodology
  • Real-world applicability & impact
  • Documentation quality
  • Presentation & feasibility

📜 Certificates & Rewards

  • Participation certificate for all
  • Merit certificates for winners
  • Research mentorship opportunities
  • Internship opportunities for top performers
